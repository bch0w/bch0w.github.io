Current Research

My current research is concerned with imaging subsurface structure below the North Island of New Zealand.

To accomplish this, I am using a technique known as adjoint tomography, which allows us to use recordings of local

earthquakes, as well as sophisticated numerical techniques to generate synthetic waveforms, in order to generate

a high resolution and accurate model of the subsurface. We hope that the information contained in this model

will help us understand the subduction interface that dives beneath the North Island, and its potential

for megathrust slip behavior, which would be immensely devastating for New Zealand.
