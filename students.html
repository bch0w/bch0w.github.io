Prospective Students

Students who are interested in working at the University of Alaska Fairbanks, surrounded by world-class research of the Geophysical Institute (GI), and world class nature of Alaska, should check out the GI's student page and the Seismology and Geodesy group's prospective students page. See below for positions I am seeking students for!

Current Openings

Funded positions for which I am seeking students or postdocs will be posted here. Positions are listed next to suggested degree track, but I am happy to work with students to determine what track is right for them. If you are interested in any of these topics (or working on any of my research interests), please reach out by email!

  1. Planetary Seismology (MSc): Starting AY2026, I will have full funding for an MSc project from NASA to work on planetary seisomlogy related to Venus. This project focuses on modelling expected ground motion for a potential future Venus lander, using numerical wave propagation solvers and estimates of Venusian tectonics (Posted June, 2024).
  2. Nuclear Explosion Monitoring (PhD or MSc): Available immediately, I have funding for one graduate student to work on seismoacoustic research broadly related to nuclear explosion monitoring. The project aims are flexible, and I am happy to work with potential students to create a project that meshes with their research interests (Posted April, 2024).
