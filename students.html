Prospective Students

Students who are interested in working and studying at the University of Alaska Fairbanks, surrounded by world-class research of the Geophysical Institute (GI), and world-class nature of Alaska, should check out information from the Department of Geosciences and the GI's Seismology and Geodesy group.



See below for positions I am seeking students for. If you are interested in working with me on any of my research interests, but do not see a related opening below, please reach out by email.

I am happy to work with UAF undergraduate students to design and oversee a project to satisfy research credits, and open to working with prospective graduate students and postdocs on collaborative proposals for student/postdoc funding opportunities.
